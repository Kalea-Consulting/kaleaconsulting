What We Do

Practical work that makes the business run better.

We work in four areas. They overlap on purpose — a clean system is worthless if no one owns it, and good operations fall apart on infrastructure that keeps breaking. We take on the parts of the business that aren't working and stay until they do.

Operations

Operations & Decision Support

When the business depends on a few people keeping everything in their heads, it can't grow and it can't rest. We put structure around how work gets done and how decisions get made, so the day-to-day runs predictably and leadership can step back from constant firefighting.

  • Process mapping and standard operating procedures for the work that matters most
  • Clear ownership and accountability so nothing falls through the cracks
  • Performance metrics and reporting that show what's actually happening
  • Decision frameworks that make the right call the obvious one
Systems

Systems & Infrastructure

The technology underneath a business should be something you never have to think about. We design, build, and harden the networks, devices, and cloud services your operation runs on — so they're secure, reliable, and ready to scale instead of becoming the thing that holds you back.

  • Network design, segmentation, and Wi-Fi that holds up under real load
  • Secure remote access for teams, sites, and devices
  • Cloud setup, backups, and continuity so a bad day doesn't become a lost week
  • Security hardening and sensible, documented configuration
Execution

Execution

Plenty of advice never makes it past the slide deck. We take ownership from diagnosis through rollout, build the automations and integrations that remove repetitive work, and don't call something done until it works in your environment and your team can run it without us in the room.

  • Workflow and lead-response automation that frees up real hours
  • Integrations that connect the tools you already use
  • Hands-on implementation, testing, and rollout
  • Documentation and handoff so the work is yours to keep
Leadership

Fractional Leadership

Some businesses need senior operations and technology leadership but aren't ready for a full-time hire. We step in as a fractional CIO or COO — setting direction, managing vendors, and keeping the roadmap moving — for a fraction of the cost and none of the lock-in.

  • Part-time operations and technology leadership, embedded in your business
  • Roadmap ownership and prioritization across competing demands
  • Vendor and tool selection without the sales-pitch bias
  • A steady hand on the wheel between bigger pieces of work

Not sure which of these you need?

Most engagements start with a conversation about what isn't working. We'll tell you straight whether it's an operations problem, a systems problem, or both — and what it would take to fix.
