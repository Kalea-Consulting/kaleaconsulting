Engagement Models

Straightforward engagements.
No surprises.

We don't do bloated retainers or vague hourly estimates that balloon. Every engagement starts with a clear scope, a clear structure, and a clear understanding of what done looks like.

Our work is priced for businesses that value outcomes over the cheapest option. Technical engagements typically start at $100 / hour — scoped to what you actually need, not padded to fill a budget. We'll tell you up front what something will cost and why, and we won't start work until we agree.

Transparency

What drives the cost.

No engagement is priced the same way. These are the factors that move the number up or down — knowing them helps you come to the conversation prepared.

Scope clarity A well-defined problem costs less to solve than an open-ended one. The more you know about what you need, the tighter we can scope it.
Technical complexity A single device configuration is different from a multi-site network build or a full automation stack. Complexity is scoped honestly, not padded.
Timeline Rushed timelines cost more. If you have flexibility, we can often find a more cost-effective path through the work.
Ongoing vs. one-time Retainer and fractional engagements carry a slight discount over pure hourly work in exchange for scheduling commitment.
